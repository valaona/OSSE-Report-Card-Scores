This site gives the EFDC team interactive access to SY2024–25 OSSE accountability and metric data across all 200 DC public and charter schools. Each page lets you filter by score tier, school framework, type, ward, and at-risk enrollment concentration — and drill into individual school performance on each metric relative to the DC statewide average.

Investment strategy context: Schools scoring 60+ are targets for access expansion. Schools scoring 40–60 serving high ED populations are the primary accelerate-improvement cohort. Schools under 20 are candidates for consolidation review.
